Hobbies

  • Hackathons

    • Joining hackathons, either in person or online, can be a thrilling hobby. These events often encourage creative, fast-paced problem-solving and give you a chance to network with others in the tech community

  • Gaming

    • Many software engineers enjoy video games, whether casual or competitive. It can be a way to unwind, and some might even enjoy learning about game development or working with game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine.

  • Learning New Frameworks and Technologies

    • Staying updated with the latest technologies in software engineering is an ongoing process. Some engineers enjoy deep-diving into new frameworks, libraries, or tools just out of curiosity or to stay ahead of trends.

  • Building Personal Projects

    • Developing side projects, whether it is a web app, mobile app, or something like a game, can be a rewarding hobby. You get to experiment with new technologies and techniques without the pressure of a work deadline.
